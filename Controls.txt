MOVEMENT:

Arrows used to move
forwards, backwards,
left and right.

Space used to Move up.

Shift used to Move down.


ROTATION:

Q and E keys used to tilt left and right.

W and S keys used to tilt forwards and backwards.

A and D keys used to turn left and right.


SPEED MODIFIERS:

Ctrl used to increase speed.

Alt used to decrease speed.
P.s. Alt must be pressed
an even amount of times
otherwise html focus could be lost.


MISCELLANEOUS:

R key used to refresh the page.

T Key for test case
